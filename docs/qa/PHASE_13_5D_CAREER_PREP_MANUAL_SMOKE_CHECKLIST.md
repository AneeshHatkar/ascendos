# Phase 13.5D Career Prep Manual Smoke Checklist

## Career route

- Visit `/career`.
- Confirm the Career dashboard renders.
- Confirm the Phase 13.5D Career prep foundation panel appears.
- Confirm the panel includes:
  - Behavioral stories
  - Questions
  - Mock interviews
  - Resume usage
  - Ready stories
  - Needs practice
  - Completed mocks
  - Source tables

## Boundary checks

- No AI interview answer is generated.
- No resume is rewritten.
- No job application is sent.
- No outreach email is sent.
- No job post scraping runs.
- No Carnos action executes.
- No voice, memory/RAG, web search, analytics, settings, or privacy feature is introduced.
